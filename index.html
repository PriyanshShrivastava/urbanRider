How to book your first ride

Subscribe from a variety of choices

01

Select your city or request us to come to your city

Never again worry about money when thinking of going for a long drive. Open urbanRider app -> select your city and get to choose your bike from a vast variety of vehicle urbanRider has. UrbanRider is a bike rental service that allows individuals to easily explore the city on two wheels.

02

Choose the type of bike you want

With a fleet of reliable and well-maintained bikes, and convenient pickup and drop-off locations, urbanRider makes it easy for riders to navigate the bustling streets of the urban landscape. Choose a bike which suits your mood from the vast variety of urbanRider vehicle colletion.

03

Select your subscription plan

urbanRider subscription service is designed for people who want the convenience and flexibility of using a bike without the commitment of owning one. With urbanRider, customers can easily rent a bike at one of their rental locations and return it at any other location.
